Crowndfund

The property crowdfunding platform allows investors to purchase a stake in a property using digital assets such as Bitcoin or Ethereum (and of course FIAT). By using blockchain technology, we ensure that transactions are secure and transparent. You can crowd one platform with your frineds or manage multiple properties. Fractional ownership, meaning you can invest as little or as much as you want. By investing in a property through the platform, the investors will earn a share of the rental income and as it open source software hosted on your own account there is no middle eating into your profits with exorbitant fees.

Use Blockchain only where it makes sense

Our platform, Buildingblock, utilizes blockchain technology in a practical way. As an example when two individuals purchase a property worth $1,000,000, the platform creates a custom token with a total supply of 1,000,000 and distributes 500,000 tokens to each party. These tokens serve as proof of ownership and entitlement to profits, but cannot be traded or exchanged on any markets. They are unique to the property and serve only as a representation of the parties' percentage ownership.

Serverless and opensource and always will be.

Our crypto-based property crowdfunding platform is built on a serverless, open-source technology and will always be free to use. This means that it is highly scalable, secure and reliable, and can be easily customized to meet the needs of the users. The platform is available to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise or resources, and the open-source nature of the platform allows for a large community of developers to contribute and improve the platform.

Being free to use, it eliminates the barriers to entry for many investors, making it accessible for a wide range of people to invest in properties using digital assets. And with the serverless architecture, you don't have to worry about managing and maintaining any servers. This makes it easy for anyone to get started with property crowdfunding without any additional costs or technical expertise. With our open-source and free crypto-based property crowdfunding platform, investing in properties has never been more accessible and convenient.
